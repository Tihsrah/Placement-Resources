(duration: 20-25 mins)

<ul>

    <li>What are the key learning in your course?</li>

<h4>Technical Interview:</h4>

(duration: 50-55 mins)
<ul>
    <li>Tell me about yourself</li>

    <li>Tell me the conditions of resource Scheduling</li>
